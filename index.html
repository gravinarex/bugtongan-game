Bugtong-Bugtong Game Instructions

Welcome to the Bugtong-Bugtong Game! Here's a quick guide on how to play:

  • Riddle Display:
    • The game will display a bugtong (riddle) in the "Riddle" section.
    • The riddle will be revealed gradually through animation.
  • Answer Input:
    • Enter your answer in the input box below the riddle.
    • Click on the "Check Answer" button to submit your answer.
  • Scoring:
    • Correct Answer: Earn 1 point.
    • Incorrect Answer: Lose a remaining guess. You start with 3 guesses.
    • Achieve a score of 10 to win the game.
  • Hints:
    • Click on the "Hint" button to receive a hint about the answer.
    • Each hint costs 2 points.
    • You have a maximum of 3 hints.
  • Get Answer (New Feature):
    • Click on the "Get Answer" button to reveal the correct answer.
    • Each use of the "Get Answer" feature costs 3 points.
    • You have a maximum of 1 usage.
  • Remaining Guesses and Hints:
    • The number of remaining guesses and hints is displayed at the bottom.
    • Use them wisely to maximize your score.
  • Time Limit:
    • You have 30 seconds to answer each bugtong.
    • The timer is displayed at the bottom.
    • If time runs out, you lose the round.
  • Sidebar:

    • You can access options like "Continue," "Menu," "Instruction," and "Quit."
  • Restart Game:
    • Click on the "Restart Game" button to start a new game.
  • Winning:
    • Reach a score of 10 to win the game.
    • If you lose all your guesses or time runs out, you lose the game.
  • Have Fun:
    • Enjoy the Bugtong-Bugtong Game! Test your wit and have a great time playing.
    • Remember, the faster and more accurate you are, the higher your score.
    • Good luck and have fun!
