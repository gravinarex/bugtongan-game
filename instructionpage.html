Bugtong - Bugtong Game



Answer Input

Scoring

Correct Answer: +1 point

Incorrect Answer: -1 remaining guess (You start with 3 guesses)

Achieve a score of 10 to win the game

Hints

Click on the "Hint" button to receive a hint about the answer.

Each hint costs 2 points.

You have a maximum of 3 hints.

Get Answer

Click on the "Get Answer" button to reveal the correct answer.

Each use of the "Get Answer" feature costs 3 points.

You have a maximum of 1 usage.

Game Features

Remaining Guesses and Hints: The number of remaining guesses and hints is displayed at the bottom.

Time Limit: You have 30 seconds to answer each bugtong. The timer is displayed at the bottom. If time runs out, you lose the round.

Sidebar: Click on the button to toggle the sidebar. You can access options like "Menu," "Instruction," and "Quit."

Restart Game: Click on the "Restart Game" button to start a new game.

Winning: Reach a score of 10 to win the game. If you lose all your guesses or time runs out, you lose the game.
